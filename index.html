Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common inquiries

What consulting services do you offer?

We provide business transformation, operational excellence, and leadership development consulting across multiple industries — helping organizations improve performance, strategy, and culture..

How does the consulting process work?

Each engagement begins with an assessment to understand your business goals and challenges. From there, we design a customized strategy, guide implementation, and measure results to ensure sustainable improvement.

What size companies do you work with?

We partner with organizations of all sizes — from small businesses to large corporations — across healthcare, manufacturing, hospitality, and other industries.

How long does it take to see results?

While every project is unique, most clients start seeing measurable impact within the first 60 to 90 days, with long-term gains following as improvements take hold.

Do you offer remote or in-person consulting?

I offer both formats according to project needs. Remote consulting is ideal for analysis and planning, while in-person is more effective for implementation and team training.

How can I contact you for a consultation?

You can contact me through the form on this page, LinkedIn, or Instagram (@rafaelirizarrypr). You can also send me an email directly at rirelevategroup@gmail.com. I respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
